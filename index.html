WhiskerDE is a fast, customizable, and user-friendly Linux Desktop Environment (DE) designed for productivity and ease of use.

Features

WhiskerDE comes with a wide range of features that are designed to enhance your desktop experience:

  • Optimized for performance and resource efficiency
  • Minimalistic yet powerful design
